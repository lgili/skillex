# Review Checklist

Use this pass after drafting or rewriting the report.

## Content

- Is the purpose obvious in the first section?
- Is the main result visible early?
- Does every section support a real technical question?
- Are assumptions and limitations explicit?
- Are open issues named clearly?

## Compression

- Can any paragraph become a table?
- Can any paragraph become a figure caption plus one sentence?
- Can repeated statements be merged?
- Can background text be reduced without losing context?
- Can obvious sentences be deleted?

## Evidence

- Does every strong claim have evidence or a stated assumption?
- Are figures, tables, and equations referenced in the text?
- Are captions informative?
- Are units, variables, and acronyms defined?

## Language

- Is the text in English?
- Is the tone technical rather than promotional?
- Are vague words replaced with measured language?
- Are long sentences split where needed?

## Closing

- Does the conclusion state the decision or technical outcome directly?
- Are risks and next actions separated from the main result?
- Would a busy engineer understand the report in a quick scan?
